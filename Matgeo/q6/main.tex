Question:\\
	Find the area of the region enclosed by the curve $y=x^2$, the $X$ axis and the ordinates $x=-2$ and $x=1$.\\

\solution \\
	The required area of the region bounded between $y=x^2$, the $X$ axis and the ordinates $x=-2$ and $x=1$ is
	\begin{align}
		\int_{-2}^1 x^2 dx = 3
	\end{align}
